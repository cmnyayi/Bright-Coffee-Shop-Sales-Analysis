# Bright-Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analyisis
Conducting Sales Analysis for the CEO to understand the performance of the shop in the last 6 months

### problems statement
The coffee shop has experienced a noticeable decline in sales performance over the past six months. As a data analyst, the goal of this project is to investigate the underlying factors contributing to the drop in revenue and identify patterns or trends that may explain the business underperformance.

### aim
The aim of the project is to analyze transactional data and deliver insights that improve business perfomance

### objectives
- To analyze sales data and identify trends over time
-	To evaluate the performance of different store locations
-	To examine customer purchasing behavior and spending patterns
- To identify peak and low-performing periods (time and day analysis)
- To provide data-driven recommendations to improve revenue

### Tools & Methodology
-	Project Planning: Canva (for presentation and reporting)
-	Brainstorming & Ideation: Miro (for structuring ideas and workflow)
-	Data Processing & Analysis: Databricks (SQL for data cleaning and transformation)
-	Data Visualization: Google Sheets (dashboard and charts)
